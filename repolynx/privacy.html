Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RepoLynx Media Publisher is used to manage and publish content to authorized social media accounts operated by RepoLynx and GitPulse.

Information We Access

The application may access information authorized through platform APIs, including account identifiers, Page identifiers, profile information, and permissions required to publish and manage content.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide authorized publishing, account connection, content management, and related functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties except where necessary to operate authorized integrations or where required by law.

Security

Authentication credentials and access tokens are handled securely and are not intentionally made publicly available.

Access Revocation

Users may revoke access to the application through their Meta account and connected-app settings.

Data Deletion

Instructions for requesting deletion of associated data are available on our Data Deletion Instructions page.

Contact

For privacy or data deletion requests, contact the project operator through the official RepoLynx or GitPulse support contact.
